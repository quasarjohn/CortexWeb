Use Cases


Here's how we've used Cortex so far.


Classify the image of your product to know the quality


An Android app that checks the quality of fruits and lets the user know if it is still fresh or not.

View project in github.

Classify the unknown


Let's say you're afraid of dogs, but you love them. And you saw the dog of your neighbor and you want to know the breed of it. With the help of Cortex, you can easily classify breed of dogs with just taking a picture of it.

When you're done taking picture of the that certain dog that you wanted to classify, the moment you send the image to the server the Cortex will instantly classify it and will return results in just a second.

Know your health in just a minute


With the help of Cortex, you also can classify if you have a certain disease. Let's say you want to know if you have sore eyes, by using Cortex you can classify if you do have sore eyes or you don't.

But that's not the limit of Cortex, as long as we do have the data we can classify any diseases. This will be useful, because you don't need to go to the hospital just to know if you have sore eyes, skin disease, etc.
